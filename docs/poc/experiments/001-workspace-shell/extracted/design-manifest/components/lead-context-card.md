# Guidance

Begin the work area with one visually quiet lead region containing a task-appropriate title and concise context. Follow it with separated content sections rather than extending the lead region into the whole page.

# Unresolved

The content, interaction, wrapping, and data model of the lead region are target-product decisions.

# Relationships

Use this as the opening region of [the workspace pattern](../screen-patterns/explanatory-workspace.md) within [workspace density](../foundations/workspace-density.md).
