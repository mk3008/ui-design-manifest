# Guidance

Show a concise state label in text. Color may reinforce meaning but must not be the only signal. Keep the badge subordinate to the record identifier and ensure its meaning remains clear in the surrounding context.

# States

Use only states supported by the product's business model. Loading, unknown, or unavailable values need an explicit local decision rather than a fabricated status.

# Relationships

Appears in the [record-list pattern](../screen-patterns/record-list.md).
