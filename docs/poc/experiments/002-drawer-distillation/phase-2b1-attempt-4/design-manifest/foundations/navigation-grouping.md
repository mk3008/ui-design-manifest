# Guidance

Place primary destinations in a vertical reading order. Do not add a generic caption to primary navigation. When a destination has children, make the parent-child relationship visible through indentation and a supplied disclosure affordance. Keep peer rows visually consistent so hierarchy, rather than decoration, explains their relationship.

A caption is optional. Use one only to introduce an owner-supplied, distinct secondary collection, and keep it quieter than actionable rows. If the owner does not supply both the secondary collection role and its caption, omit the caption and its dependent collection treatment.

# Unresolved

Destination taxonomy, collection roles, captions, and record-specific meaning are owner inputs.
