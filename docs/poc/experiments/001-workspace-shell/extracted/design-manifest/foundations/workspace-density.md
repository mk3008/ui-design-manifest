# Guidance

Keep the workspace calm and utilitarian: light surfaces, modest separation between regions, compact but readable controls, and clear type hierarchy. Use a single pale lead area to introduce the current content before denser explanatory sections.

# Evidence

E-001 visibly separates header, navigation, main background, lead card, section labels, headings, body copy, and small keyword chips without a decorative or promotional treatment.

# Unresolved

Exact values, contrast measurements, typography families, motion, and state treatments beyond those visible in E-001 are not specified.
