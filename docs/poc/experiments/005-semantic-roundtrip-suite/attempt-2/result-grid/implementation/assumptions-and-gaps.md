# Assumptions and gaps

## Assumptions

- `I` and `II` are arbitrary neutral fixture instances. They demonstrate repeated structure only; they do not assert record count, order, identity, or product meaning.
- The marker tokens demonstrate entry association only. They do not communicate state, status, value, or update behavior.

## Gaps retained

- No product-owned header value, record collection, record identity, metadata meaning, marker meaning, destination, or interaction is supplied.
- Sorting, pagination, selection, activation, navigation, loading, empty and failure states, update triggers, and responsive behavior remain omitted.
- Generated evidence is unassessed for preservation until independent source-blind re-extraction.
