# Guidance

Keep primary identifiers, status, and the next likely action visible during scanning. Use spacing and grouping to separate records; do not rely on decorative containers for every field. Secondary details may be quieter but must remain readable.

# Unresolved

No numeric spacing scale or viewport breakpoint is prescribed. Derive those choices from the current product implementation and observed content.
