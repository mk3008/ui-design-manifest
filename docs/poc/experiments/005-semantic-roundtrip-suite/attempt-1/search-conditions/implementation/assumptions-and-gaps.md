# Assumptions and gaps

- The static evidence represents slot order, visible association, and submit ownership only.
- The first and second selection controls have no declared product relationship.
- No product labels, choices, defaults, validation, submission outcome, errors, result context, state source, or update trigger is established.
- The optional selection is rendered only as the declared neutral unchecked fixture.
- Wide and narrow captures record the same bounded static state; they do not establish responsive behavior.
