# Guidance

Begin the Overview work area with one visually quieter lead card containing a section title, a concise explanatory line, and small topic cues. Follow it with separated content sections rather than extending the card into the entire page.

# Evidence

E-001 and E-002 show the same lead card at the beginning of the main area, containing the title `UI Contract Editor`, a Japanese explanatory line, and six compact keyword cues.

# Unresolved

The meaning, interaction, wrapping behavior, and data source of the cues were not inspected.
