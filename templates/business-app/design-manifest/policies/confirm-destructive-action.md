# Guidance

Separate a destructive action from routine actions and name its consequence before execution. Ask for confirmation when the outcome cannot be readily undone. Preserve keyboard access, focus clarity, and a safe cancellation path.

# Unresolved

The business meaning of irreversible and any authorization checks belong to the product requirements, not this example.
